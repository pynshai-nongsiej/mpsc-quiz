Directions: (Q.No. 46-50) In these questions, the following sentence have been split into four segments. Identify the segment that contains a grammatical error. (ERROR IDENTIFICATION)

46. I reach to my house (a) / as soon as (b) / I will send the driver (c) / to pick you up (d)
*Error: (a) 'reach' does not take 'to' when followed by a direct object like 'my house' (it should be "I reach my house").*
a) I reach to my house (Answer)
b) as soon as
c) I will send the driver
d) to pick you up

47. Declining in the country (a) / Since 2000 (b) / Poor nutrition have been (c) / People face hunger and (d)
*Error: (c) The subject 'Poor nutrition' is singular, so the verb should be 'has been' (Poor nutrition has been...).*
a) Declining in the country
b) Since 2000
c) Poor nutrition have been (Answer)
d) People face hunger and

48. had you (a) / lost our lives (b) / not reached in time (c) / we will have (d)
*Error: (d) This is an inverted third conditional. The main clause requires 'would have' (we would have lost our lives). The auxiliary 'will' is incorrect.*
a) had you
b) lost our lives
c) not reached in time
d) we will have (Answer)

49. each of the survivors of the pandemic (a) / free psychological consultation to ease their (b) / have been offered (c) / trauma, by some of the top consultants (d)
*Error: (c) The subject is 'each' (singular), so the verb should be 'has been offered'.*
a) each of the survivors of the pandemic
b) free psychological consultation to ease their
c) have been offered (Answer)
d) trauma, by some of the top consultants

50. encouraging healthy competition (a) / between the students (b) / was one of the objectives (c) / of the college (d)
*No Error: The sentence is grammatically correct as written.*
a) encouraging healthy competition
b) between the students
c) was one of the objectives
d) of the college (Answer: Assuming D is the part to select when no error is found in a, b, or c)
