*** Directions: ERROR DETECTION (Q. Nos. 43-46) ***

43. The airline company (A) / will obliged to give refund (B)/ if the flight is cancelled (C)/ No error (D).
 a) A
 b) B
 c) C
 d) D
[Answer: (b)] (Error: Should be "will **be** obliged to give refund")

44. There wasn't no bus service (A)/ tomorrow because of (B)/ the union strike (C)/ No error (D).
 a) A
 b) B
 c) C
 d) D
[Answer: (a)] (Error: Double negative. Should be "There wasn't **any** bus service" or "There was **no** bus service")

45. Her disease seem (A)/ to get worse (B)/ day by day (C)/ No error (D).
 a) A
 b) B
 c) C
 d) D
[Answer: (a)] (Error: Subject-verb agreement. Should be "Her disease **seems**")

46. If the rain (A) / doesn't let up soon (B)/ the river might climb up (C)/No error (D).
 a) A
 b) B
 c) C
 d) D
[Answer: (c)] (Error: "Climb up" is an unidiomatic/incorrect verb choice for a river. Should be "the river might **rise**")
