
Directions (Q. Nos. 1-15): Choose the correct options to fill in the gaps.

2. Health is too important to be __________
(A) discarded
(B) neglected - CORRECT ANSWER (To fail to care for something)

(A) peculiar
(B) proper
(C) fair - CORRECT ANSWER (Meaning considerable/reasonable)
(D) abundant

4. The passengers of the flight were afraid but the captain __________ them that they were safe.
(A) counselled
(B) promised
(C) advised
(D) assured - CORRECT ANSWER (To give a positive declaration to remove doubt)

5. I have not seen them __________ a week.
(A) within
(B) since
(C) for - CORRECT ANSWER (Used with a period of time)
(D) from

6. Do you know __________ ?
(A) where she comes from - CORRECT ANSWER (Correct word order for an indirect question)
(B) where does she come from
(C) where from she comes
(D) from where does she come

7. __________ you hear the President's speech?
(A) Has
(B) Have
(C) Had
(D) Did - CORRECT ANSWER (Simple past auxiliary for a completed action)

8. How much longer __________ this book?
(A) will you be needing - CORRECT ANSWER (Future continuous for asking the duration of a need)
(B) you are needing
(C) will you have needed
(D) have you needed

9. The building was so old and dilapidated that it was not __________
(A) habitat
(B) habitation
(C) habitable - CORRECT ANSWER (Adjective meaning fit to live in)
(D) habitant

10. Polyester is more __________ than cotton.
(A) duration
(B) durable - CORRECT ANSWER (Adjective meaning long-lasting)
(C) durably
(D) durability

11. The proud king turned a deaf ear to the __________ of his wise counsellors.
(A) advices
(B) advise
(C) advises
(D) advice - CORRECT ANSWER (Uncountable noun)

12. She has only __________ friends.
(A) more
(B) fewer
(C) a few - CORRECT ANSWER (Used for a small, positive number of count nouns)
(D) less

13. The police pushed the people back to make __________ for the Minister's car.
(A) passage
(B) way - CORRECT ANSWER (Idiom: "make way")
(C) place
(D) area

14. I have made a rough __________ of the report.
(A) drape
(B) drought
(C) draught
(D) draft - CORRECT ANSWER (A preliminary version of a document)

15. We __________ not hurry, we have got plenty of time.
(A) need - CORRECT ANSWER (Modal verb: "need not" expresses lack of necessity)
(B) could
(C) must
(D) should
