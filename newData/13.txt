Directions (Q1-20): In the following questions, sentences are given with blanks to be filled with an appropriate and suitable word. Four alternatives are suggested for each question. Choose the correct alternatives out of the four.

1. He finds delight _______ books.
a) from
b) in
c) through
d) with
Answer: b) in

2. Do not discriminate _______ the poor.
a) against
b) about
c) by
d) with
Answer: a) against

3. The truth at last dawned _______ him.
a) to
b) by
c) for
d) on
Answer: d) on

4. Will you give me _______ water?
a) little
b) much
c) many
d) some
Answer: d) some

5. I took shelter in the _______ house.
a) nearby
b) nearest
c) near
d) nearer
Answer: a) nearby

6. What is the _______ news?
a) late
b) lately
c) latest
d) later
Answer: c) latest

7. She is a good student _______ she is sometimes careless.
a) except
b) excepting
c) except that
d) except at.
Answer: c) except that

8. He received _______ urgent message.
a) the
b) an
c) a
d) that
Answer: b) an

9. He always speaks the truth. _______ I like him.
a) Therefore
b) although
c) Besides
d) Neverthless
Answer: a) Therefore

10. I am in _______ of your advice.
a) demand
b) want
c) requirement
d) need
Answer: d) need

11. There is no _______ when he will come.
a) knowledge
b) know
c) knowing
d) known
Answer: a) knowledge (Note: The complete and correct idiom would be "There is no knowing...")

12. I'm going _______ next month.
a) foreign
b) international
c) outside
d) abroad
Answer: d) abroad

13. You have finished your homework, _______?
a) didn't you?
b) haven't you?
c) hasn't you?
d) wasn't it?
Answer: b) haven't you?

14. Your sister is a nurse, _______?
a) isn't it?
b) wasn't it?
c) wasn't she?
d) isn't she?
Answer: d) isn't she?

15. My father _______ home last week.
a) left off
b) went back
c) made out
d) came down
Answer: b) went back (Assuming the question means 'returned home' or 'went to his residence'.)

16. He will have to _______ his mistakes.
a) pay to
b) pay off
c) pay for
d) pay over
Answer: c) pay for

17. The thieves _______ in a stolen jeep.
a) made out
b) made up
c) made off
d) made over
Answer: c) made off

18. He is not _______ well.
a) staying
b) showing
c) keeping
d) maintaining
Answer: c) keeping

19. I cannot conceive _______ such cruelty.
a) on
b) of
c) about
d) for
Answer: b) of

20. The man was shocked because _______ his son was a thief.
a) he was told
b) he was telling
c) he tried to tell
d) he once told.
Answer: a) he was told (The context implies the man *received* the shocking information.)

Directions: (Q No. 21-30) In these questions out of the four alternatives given, choose the one which best expresses the meaning of the underlined word (Synonyms).

c) doubtful
b) uncertain
d) detached.
Answer: a) obscure (While 'uncertain' and 'doubtful' are close, 'obscure' or 'vague' are the closest synonyms for 'ambiguous' meaning open to more than one interpretation.)

28. The character in the novel is a thorough **profligate**.
a) moral person
b) a criminal

29. After being rude to her mother, the young girl is **remorseful**.

30. He **tended** the saplings with great care.

Directions: (Q No. 41-50) In these questions out of the four alternatives given choose the one which is opposite in meaning of the underlined word (Antonyms).

41. Her dress is too **garish** for my liking.

b) gaudy
c) sober
d) transparent.
Answer: c) sober

42. There is a **derelict** building on the way.

b) stately
c) horrible
d) desolate.
Answer: b) stately (Antonym of 'derelict' (in a very poor condition as a result of disuse and neglect) would be 'stately' or 'magnificent'. 'Stately' implies being in good, impressive condition.)
