*** DIRECTIONS: ERROR DETECTION (Q. Nos. 1-5) ***

1. A month (A)/has passed (B)/since he came here (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (D)]

2. He both (A) / obtained (B) / a prize and a scholarship (C)/No error (D)
[Answer: (A)] (The correct structure is: He obtained both a prize and a scholarship.)

3. She prevented (A)/me in visiting (B)/my mother (C)/No error (D)
[Answer: (B)] (The correct preposition is 'from': prevented me from visiting.)

4. We warned her (A) / of the danger (B)/by pickpockets (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (C)] (The correct preposition is 'from' or 'against': of the danger from/against pickpockets.)

5. I impressed (A) / on her the value (B) / of cleanliness (C)/No error (D)
[Answer: (D)]
