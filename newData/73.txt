*** Directions: ERROR DETECTION (Q. Nos. 31-35) ***

31. The cows are (a)/running (b)/against the fence (c) / No error (d)
[Answer: (c)] (Error: The preposition should be 'along' or 'near', not 'against'.)

32. I am studying (a) / hard for the (b) / last three months (c)/No error (d)
[Answer: (a)] (Error: Should be 'I have been studying' to indicate an action continuing up to the present.)

33. An honorary degree (a) / was conferred (b)/on the President (c) / No error (d)
[Answer: (d)]

34. Tina insisted (a) / him to went (b) / back home (c) / No error (d)
[Answer: (b)] (Error: Should be 'insisted on his going' or 'insisted that he go'.)

35. The Police accused (a) / Vishal with theft (b)/and murder (c) / No error (d)
[Answer: (b)] (Error: The correct preposition is 'of': 'accused Vishal of theft'.)

*** Directions: SENTENCE REARRANGEMENT (Q. Nos. 36-40) ***

36. A. She's putting them on the trolley
B. She went to the mall
C. She's taking the food packets
D. She's paying for the items at the counter.
a) BCAD
b) BACD
c) BDCA
d) BDAC
[Answer: (a)] (Correct sequence: B. Went to the mall -> C. Taking the food packets -> A. Putting them on the trolley -> D. Paying at the counter.)

37. A. First aid could be vital in saving someone's life
B. Every year many accidents occur
C. With some care accidents can be prevented
D. In case of an accident, knowing first aid can be of great help
a) BADC
b) BCAD
c) DCBA
d) ABCD
[Answer: (b)] (Correct sequence: B. Accidents occur -> C. Accidents can be prevented -> A. First aid is vital (if they happen) -> D. Knowing first aid is a great help (elaboration).)

38. A. The bus is going from the city to the University
B. The bus is now stuck in front of the car
C. The bus is waiting at the bus stop for the passengers
D. The bus is moving slowly from the city to its destination
a) ABCD
b) BCDA
c) CABD
d) DBCA
[Answer: (c)] (Correct sequence: C. Waiting -> A. Going from city to Uni -> B. Stuck -> D. Moving slowly to its destination.)

39. A. Do not break blisters or apply anything on the burns
B. Immediately cool the area with cold water for a few minutes
C. A burn is caused by dry heat, steam, hot water or oil
D. A burn should be covered with a sterile or clean cloth or bandage
a) BACD
b) CBAD
c) CBDA
d) ABCD
[Answer: (b)] (Correct sequence: C. Definition/Cause -> B. First action (cool) -> A. Follow-up instruction (don't break blisters) -> D. Final action (cover).)

40. A. You must water the plant regularly
B. You must place the pot near the sunlight
C. You must cover the pot with plastic wrap for a few days
D. You must plant the seed in the pot first.
a) CDBA
b) CBDA
c) DBCA
d) BACD
[Answer: (c)] (Correct sequence: D. Plant the seed -> B. Place near sunlight -> C. Cover with plastic wrap (for germination) -> A. Water regularly (ongoing).)
