Directions: In question 41-45, some parts of the given sentences have errors and some are correct. Find out which part of the sentences has an error and if a sentence is free from errors, your answer is (d) No error.

41. it is advisable (a) / to go at (b) / the documents carefully (c) / No error (d)
Answer: (b) [Error: 'go at' should be 'go through']

42. I have been (a) / waiting for you over (b)/ for an hour (c) / No error (d)
Answer: (b) [Error: 'over' is redundant]

43. It will be good (a) / for your health if you can (b) / come to the office by foot (c) / No error (d)
Answer: (c) [Error: 'by foot' should be 'on foot']

44. The thief entered (a) / to the premises (b)/ without being seen (c) / No error (d)
Answer: (b) [Error: 'entered to' is incorrect. 'Entered' is transitive and does not need a preposition here]

45. There is a lacking (a)/of skilled labourers (b)/now a days (c) / No error (d)
Answer: (a) [Error: 'a lacking' should be 'a lack']
