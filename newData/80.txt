31. In many countries, people drive _______ the left.
a) in

c) on (Answer - Idiomatic phrase: "drive on the left")

32. It is risky to enter _______ partnership with people you do not know very well.
a) to
b) into (Answer - "Enter into" a contract/agreement/partnership)
c) with
d) on

33. His parents were transferred to Tura _______ he was five years old.
a) when (Answer - Used for a specific point in time)
b) since
c) while
d) until

34. John has been waiting here _______ morning.
a) for
b) since (Answer - Used to indicate a starting point in time for an action continuing into the present)
c) at
d) from

a) prompted (Answer - Meaning caused or incited)
b) promoted
c) allowed
d) forced

36. There is nothing to do, _______?

d) is it

b) isn't it
c) won't you
d) wouldn't you

38. He told me that he was tired, _______ he didn't want to go out.
a) so (Answer - Used to show consequence)
b) even though
c) because
d) but

a) between
b) among (Answer - Used for three or more people/things)
c) with
d) for
