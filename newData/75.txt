Directions: (Q.No. 1 to 5) ERROR SPOTTING

1. Give the package to whom ever (a) / has the authority (b)/ to sign for it (c) / No error (d)
Error: (a) - Should be "whoever" (subject pronoun).

2. In our opinion that girl is (a) / enough beautiful (b)/ to be a movie star (c) / No error (d)
Error: (b) - Should be "beautiful enough" (adjective comes before 'enough').

3. Our friends got a bank loan (a) / for to buy (b) / a new car (c) / No error (d)
Error: (b) - Should be "to buy" (infinitive of purpose).

4. The flag is risen at 6.30 (a) / every morning (b)/ without fail (c) / No error (d)
Error: (a) - Should be "The flag is raised" (transitive verb in passive voice) or "The flag rises" (intransitive verb in simple present).

5. The refrigerator is too old (a) / to keep things (b) / at a proper temperature (c) / No error (d)
Answer: (d) - No error.

6. Doesn't the cat want _______ milk?
a) some (Answer - Used in an anticipated positive response or a request/offer)

a) kind (Answer - Correct structure is "as + adjective + as")
b) kinder
c) kindest
d) most kind

8. I have _______ things to do.
a) so much
b) very little
c) too many
d) a lot (Answer - The best general fit for plural countable noun 'things')

9. He looks _______ his grandmother.
a) as
b) by
c) like (Answer - Correct phrasal verb for similarity)
d) same

10. It was _______ to catch the plane.
a) late
b) so late
c) very late
d) too late (Answer - Implies excessive lateness, preventing the action)

11. Lydia is _______ in our group about exam results.
a) the more nervous
b) the most nervous (Answer - Superlative degree indicated by "in our group")
c) not as nervous as
d) as nervous as

12. Ted wishes that he _______ his job last month.
a) have not lost
b) is not losing
c) had not lost (Answer - Past unreal condition/wish uses Past Perfect)
d) were not lost

13. I am opposed to _______ to war.
a) be gone
b) be going
c) have been gone
d) going (Answer - "Opposed to" is followed by a gerund)

14. Your father is a teacher, _______?
a) isn't it
b) isn't he (Answer - Correct question tag for the subject 'father')
c) wasn't it
d) was it not

15. He refused _______ my plea.
a) considering
b) having to consider
c) consideration
d) to consider (Answer - The verb 'refuse' takes the infinitive)
