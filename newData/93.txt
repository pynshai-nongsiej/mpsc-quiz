*** Directions: ERROR DETECTION (Q. No. 1-10) ***
(Find out which part of a sentence a, b, c has an error. If there is no error then d is the answer.)

1. Even such a strong man (A) / like him (B) / cannot lift the box (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (B)] (Error: Should be "as him" for the correct correlative "such...as".)

2. Clark spent many years (A) / of his studies (B)/exploring the meaning of the life (C)/No error (D)
[Answer: (C)] (Error: Should be "of life" as the word 'life' is used in a general sense.)

3. We were (A) / no interested (B) / in what they were selling (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (B)] (Error: Should be "not interested". 'No' is incorrect as an adverb here.)

4. Too great (A) / a variety of subjects (B) / strain the mind (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (C)] (Error: The subject "a variety" is singular, so the verb must be singular: "strains the mind".)

5. The ship (A) / was drowned (B) / in the sea (C)/No error (D)
[Answer: (B)] (Error: 'Drowned' is used for animate objects. For a ship, the verb should be "sank" or "was sunk".)

6. The rain (A) / was accompanied (B) / by a hailstorm (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (D)]

7. Scarcely (A) / had she gone (B) / than he arrived (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (C)] (Error: The correct correlative for "Scarcely" is "when", not "than".)

8. You are (A) / a mechanic (B) / isn't it? (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (C)] (Error: The correct question tag for "You are" is "aren't you?")

9. Donald loves (A) / eating pizza (B) / and to watch television (C) / No error (D)
[Answer: (C)] (Error: Lack of parallel structure. Should be "and watching television" to match "eating pizza".)

10. He spoke (A) / a lie and said me (B) / a liar (C)/ No error (D)
[Answer: (B)] (Error: Should be "He told a lie" and "told me" or "called me" a liar.)
