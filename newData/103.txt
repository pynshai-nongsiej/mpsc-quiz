*** SECTION-I: CHOOSE THE CORRECT MEANING FOR THE WORDS AND PHRASES GIVEN BELOW (Q. Nos. 1-10) ***

1. Anything which cannot be seen is
 a) Visible
 c) Invisible
 b) Invincible
 d) Inaudible
[Answer: (c)]

2. That which cannot be corrected is
 a) Unchangeable
 c) Incorrect
 b) Uncorrectable
 d) Incorrigible
[Answer: (d)]

3. Oligarchy means
 a) Government by a king
 b) Government by a few
 c) Government by the people
 d) Government by a tyrant
[Answer: (b)]

4. A misanthropist is
 a) A person who studies mankind
 b) A person who loves mankind
 c) A person who hates mankind
 d) A person who believes in mankind
[Answer: (c)]

5. The study of the heavenly bodies is called
 a) Astrology
 c) Astronomy
 b) Astrophysics
 d) Astronomer
[Answer: (c)]

6. "Enmity" also means
 a) Love
 c) Hostility
 b) Friendship
 d) Generosity
[Answer: (c)]

7. A group of bees is also called
 a) A flight of bees
 c) A swarm of bees
 b) A shower of bees
 d) A grove of bees
[Answer: (c)]

8. Another word for "anger" is
 a) Gallant
 c) Wrath
 b) Hate
 d) Polite
[Answer: (c)]

9. Choose the correct comparison
 a) As bright as day
 b) As light as day
 c) As happy as day
 d) As shiny as day
[Answer: (a)]

10. Choose the correct comparison
 a) As noisy as lightning
 b) As loud as lightning
 c) As quick as lightning
 d) As white as lightning
[Answer: (c)]

*** SECTION-III: FILL IN THE BLANKS (Q. Nos. 16-25) ***

16. I caught him by ____ left arm.
 a) A
 c) The
 b) An
 d) Zero article
[Answer: (c)]

17. Babar ____ Brave defeated Rana Sanga.
 a) The
 c) An
 b) A
 d) Zero article
[Answer: (a)] (Conventional title/epithet usage is "Babar the Brave")

18. They are very proud ____ their success.
 a) At
 c) Of
 b) About
 d) Through
[Answer: (c)]

19. We are sorry ____ your mother's illness.
 a) For
 c) About
 b) At
 d) Because
[Answer: (c)]

20. Everest is the ____ mountain in the world.
 a) Most high
 c) High
 b) Highest
 d) More high
[Answer: (b)]

21. Her dress is ____ than mine.
 a) More clean
 c) Cleanest
 b) Cleaner
 d) Most clean
[Answer: (b)]

22. Susan works very ____.
 a) Harder
 c) Hardly
 b) Hardest
 d) Hard
[Answer: (d)]

23. ____ soldier has a gun in his hand.
 a) All
 c) Each
 b) Every
 d) Some
[Answer: (b)]

24. Neither the father nor the sons ____ going to prepare dinner tonight.
 a) Is
 c) Will be
 b) Are
 d) Were
[Answer: (b)] (Verb agrees with the closest subject, 'the sons', which is plural)

25. Not only the teacher but the students also ____ there.
 a) Was
 c) Were
 b) Is
 d) Will be
[Answer: (c)] (Verb agrees with the closest subject, 'the students', which is plural)
