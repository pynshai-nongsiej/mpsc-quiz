*** SECTION-I: FILL IN THE GAPS ***

1. __________ Persian Gulf is rich in oil.
 A) A
 B) An
 C) The
 D) Zero article
[Answer: (C) The]

2. He is the boy __________ stole my purse.
 A) Who
 B) Whom
 C) That
 D) Which
[Answer: (A) Who]

3. My father __________ to office at 10 a.m daily.
 A) Go
 B) Went
 C) Goes
 D) Will go
[Answer: (C) Goes]

4. You don't like sugar in your coffee, __________?
 A) Don't you?
 B) Do you?
 C) Didn't you?
 D) Isn't it?
[Answer: (B) Do you?]

5. She was __________ because all her plans failed.
 A) Ecstatic
 B) Considerate
 C) Dejected
 D) Fascinated
[Answer: (C) Dejected]

[Answer: (C) Dangerous]

 C) Exasperate

[Answer: (A) Erase]

8. Pensive means:
 A) Reflective
 B) Moving
 C) Dishonourable
 D) Harsh
[Answer: (A) Reflective]

9. Antithesis means:
 A) Similar
 B) Reverse
 C) Plentiful
 D) Peaceful
[Answer: (B) Reverse]

10. Unwieldy means:
 A) Simple
 B) Easy
 C) Awkward

[Answer: (C) Awkward]

*** SECTION-III: CORRECT SENTENCE ***
(Of the four sentences given, only one is correct. Choose the correct answer.)

11.

 C) Please do not deny an offer of the Chairman
 D) Please do not deny an offer made of the Chairman
[Answer: (B) Please do not refuse an offer made by the Chairman]

 B) No sooner I reached the station, than the train left
 C) No sooner did I reach the station, when the train left
 D) No sooner I reached the station, when the train left
[Answer: (A) No sooner did I reach the station, than the train left]

13.
 A) He asked me that why I was sad.
 B) He asked me why I was sad.
 C) He asked me why I am sad.
 D) He asked me why I have been sad.
[Answer: (B) He asked me why I was sad.]

14.
 A) The priest told the woman that her son would recover
 B) The priest asked the woman that her son would recover
 C) The priest promised the woman that her son would recover
 D) The priest hoped or assured the woman that her son would recover
[Answer: (A) The priest told the woman that her son would recover]

15.
 A) The terrorists made a vain attempt to blow over the bridge
 B) The terrorists made a vain attempt to blow down the bridge
 C) The terrorists made a vain attempt to blow up the bridge
 D) The terrorists made a vain attempt to blow on the bridge
[Answer: (C) The terrorists made a vain attempt to blow up the bridge]

[Answer: (A) The building was so old that it was not habitable]

17.

18.

[Answer: (B) I asked him who he was.]

19.

20.
